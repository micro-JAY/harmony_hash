# Harmony Hash: Global Progression Library

This library uses Roman Numeral notation (I, ii, iii, IV, V, vi, vii°).

- **Uppercase** = Major chord
- **Lowercase** = Minor chord
- **°** = Diminished
- **/** = Over/Slash chord (e.g., I/III is a Major 1 chord with the 3rd in the bass)

---

## 1. Major Key Progressions (Bright, Soulful, Standard)

### The Foundations (Rock, Pop, Folk)

- **The "Primary" Loop:** I – IV – V
- **The Rock Standard:** I – bVII – IV
- **The "Axis" (Pop Standard):** I – V – vi – IV
- **The "Pachelbel" Canon:** I – V – vi – iii – IV – I – IV – V
- **The Sensitive Pop Loop:** I – IV – vi – V
- **The "50s" (Doo-Wop):** I – vi – IV – V
- **The Plagal Loop:** I – IV – I – V
- **Uplifting Climb:** I – ii – iii – IV
- **The Hopeful Step:** I – iii – IV – V
- **The "Wild Thing" Energy:** I – IV – V – IV

### Jazz & R&B Fundamentals

- **The 2-5-1 (The King):** ii – V – I
- **The Pop-Jazz Variant:** I – IV – ii – V
- **The Jazz Turnaround:** I – vi – ii – V
- **The Extended Turnaround:** iii – vi – ii – V
- **The "Stoned" R&B Loop:** Imaj7 – IVmaj7
- **The "Misty" Movement:** Imaj7 – vi7 – ii7 – V7
- **The Mediant Shift:** I – III7 – IV

### Gospel & Soul Movement

- **The "Sunday Morning" Walk:** I – I/III – IV – V
- **The "Preacher" Cadence:** IV – IVm – I
- **The Neo-Soul "Rub":** ii – vii° – I – iii – IV
- **Circular Soul:** I – VI7 – ii – V
- **The "Soulful Descent":** I – V/vii – vi – V
- **The "Passing" Diminished:** I – #I°7 – ii – V

## 2. Minor Tonality Progressions (Moody, Dark, Emotional)

> Note: We blend Natural and Harmonic minor here for professional results.

### The Essentials (Pop & Rock)

- **The "Hit the Road" Descent:** i – VII – VI – V *(Andalusian Cadence)*
- **The "Sensitive Minor" Loop:** i – VI – III – VII
- **The "Watchtower":** i – VII – VI – VII
- **The Melancholy Walk:** i – VI – III – VII
- **The Moody Pop Cycle:** i – iv – VI – V
- **The "Sultans" Movement:** i – VII – VI – III
- **The "Creep" Minor Turn:** i – III – IV – iv *(Borrowed major IV to minor iv)*

### R&B & Soul Minor Loops

- **The "Infinite Minor":** i – VI – VII
- **The Soulful Minor 1-4:** i – IV *(Dorian feel, e.g., Am → D)*
- **The Minor "Walk-up":** i – VII – III – IV
- **The Dark R&B Loop:** i – v – VI – VII
- **The "Smooth Operator" Change:** im7 – iim7 – bIIImaj7 – iim7
- **The Neo-Soul Minor:** i9 – iv11 – bVII13 – bIIImaj9

### Harmonic/Classical Minor (Strong Pull)

- **The Dramatic Cadence:** i – iv – V – i
- **The Spanish Vamp:** i – bII – III – bII
- **The Tense Resolution:** ii° – V – i
- **The "Vampire" Cycle:** i – V7

### Jazz Minor (Sophisticated)

- **Minor 2-5-1:** ii° – V7 – i
- **The Deceptive Minor:** i – bVI – ii° – V
- **Minor Jazz Turnaround:** i – vi° – ii° – V
- **The "Blue Minor":** im7 – IV7 *(Dorian-influenced Jazz)*

## 3. Modal & "Vibe" Progressions (Exotic, Floating)

### Dorian (The "Cool" Funk)

- **The Santana Vamp:** im – IV
- **Funk Foundation:** im – ii – IV
- **The "So What" Change:** im7 – iim7

### Mixolydian (The "Greasy" Rock/Soul)

- **Classic Rocker:** I – bVII – IV
- **The Southern Soul:** I – IV – bVII
- **The "Clocks" Loop:** I – v – ii

### Lydian (The "Ethereal" Dream)

- **Floating Lydian:** I – II
- **Space Cinematic:** I – II – IV – I

### Phrygian (The "Aggressive" Dark)

- **The Metal/Dark-Trap:** i – bII
- **The "Siren" Tension:** i – bII – i – bIII

### Locrian (The "Forbidden" / Unstable)

- **The Dark Abyss:** i° – bII
- **Locrian Tension:** i° – bV – bII
- **The Glitch Loop:** i° – IVm – bII

## 4. Advanced "Crash Out" & Passing Progressions

> These use chromatic movement and secondary dominants for high tension.

- **The Tritone Sub:** ii – bII7 – I *(The bII7 replaces the V)*
- **The "Backdoor" Exit:** iv – bVII – I *(Common in Jazz/R&B)*
- **Chromatic Walkdown:** I – bVII – VI – bVI
- **The "Secondary" Pull:** I – V/ii – ii – V
- **The "Coltrane" Mediant:** I – bVI – bIV *(Ultra-modern Jazz movement)*
